INFORMED CONSENT FORM

User Experience Testing with Biometric Analysis

Purpose of the Study

You are being invited to participate in a research study that aims to understand user experience through biometric analysis and behavioral observation.

What Will Happen

During this study, you will be asked to complete tasks while we collect the following data:

  • Facial expressions through camera recording for emotion analysis
  • Screen interactions and navigation patterns
  • Task completion times and success rates
Data Privacy & Security

All biometric data will be encrypted and stored securely. Your facial emotion data will be processed using AI algorithms and anonymized immediately after analysis. No identifying information will be linked to your biometric data.

Your Rights
  • Participation is voluntary
  • You may withdraw at any time
  • You can request data deletion
  • You can pause recording at any time
Biometric Data Consent

By proceeding, you explicitly consent to the collection and analysis of your facial expression data for research purposes only.
